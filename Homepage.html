ABOUT THE PRODUCT


Our product analyses the road traffic using a static surveillance traffic cameras and detects the occurrence of road accidents.
Furthermore, depending on the severity of the accident, alert the nearby hospitals and police station in order to ensure that timely medical and legal aid is provided to the injured victim. This web-based product finds utmost importance in today's real world where deaths due to road accidents caused by untimely medical aid and negligent driving are on the rise.

Key Features of our product:

RESPONSIVE

ADS provides a quick and an immediate response with an almost negligible time delay thereby alerting the nearest legal and medical authorities.

INEXPENSIVE

We believe that Money shouldn't be an inconvenience to families when it comes to medical emergencies. Our vision is that our product reaches to all.

DESIGN

The product has been designed to provide a user friendly interface to it's clients along with 24/7 dedicated customer service support.

SUPPORT

We provide 24/7 constant camera surveillance as well in order to avoid any false alarms or in case the accidents go undetected.
